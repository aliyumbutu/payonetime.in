About Us

Meet Md Shahab Anzar and Aliyu M Butu, the co-founders driving the vision of Payonetime. Motivated by a shared vision for financial inclusivity, they embarked on a mission to revolutionize the student lending landscape.

Their entrepreneurial journey commenced with a keen observation of the financial constraints faced by students in India. From textbooks to unforeseen medical expenses, traditional banking avenues often fell short in catering to these urgent needs, especially for students lacking established credit histories.

Payonetime resolves this challenge by providing streamlined, expedited loans tailored specifically for students. Say goodbye to cumbersome collateral requirements, income verification hassles, and lengthy approval processes. With Payonetime, accessing timely financial assistance becomes a seamless experience, empowering students to tackle financial hurdles with ease.
